How to Reach ?

For our out of town guests, fly into the Las Vegas International Airport (airport code LVS).

We have arranged for two hotel room blocks at The Caesars Palace and The Linq Hotel (hotel across the street). Please note Caesars Palace is located in the middle of the strip, surrounded by many other hotel options if you desire. Additionally, if you go directly to either Caesars or the Linq sites, there may be better deals.

Las Vegas springweather is typically hot (high in high 80s and low in high 60s). The ceremony will be outside and the reception will be inside.

If you have any questions about any of the above, feel free to email us at hello@w3nuts.co.uk
