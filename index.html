Engagement Story

If anyone knows us, they know that Brad and I don't miss any meals and love to eat. Living in Chicago, there are so many great options and really, it's like pick your poison.

With this in mind, we decided to try RPM Steak based on reviews and their menu, which certainly didn't disappoint. Despite having reservations we still had to wait 15 minutes for a table. Once we were seated, our eyes were much bigger than our stomachs but we loved every minute of our food. Of course, we ended the evening with dessert which I live for. After eating over half of our dessert, did I mention I really like dessert, I literally rolled out of my seat as we headed to coat check to retrieve our coats. During our walk, Brad recommended we do a cocktails at The Ritz Carlton - which was one of our first date places. To be honest, I was stuffed beyond belief but since this was our first night together after Christmas, I figured why not and I'm so glad that I did. Once we made our way to the hotel lobby, picked a great location and ordered drinks, Brad headed to the bathroom. Meanwhile, I was checking my email and of course, the gram.

When Brad returned, he looked me dead in my eyes and started to share his feels about me, fast forward a few secs, he was down-on-bended-knee. I can't even remember what he said and I think I just blurted out "yes" before he could even finish in true Andrews fashion, we go after what we want. It happened so fast, the details are really a blur.

Thankfully, we weren't the only ones in the lobby and a woman, who become our paparazzi, immediately came to our table and asked us if we just got engaged. We said yes, gave her Brad's phone and credit her for all of the impromptu engagement photos that we have. She even recommended that Brad re-proposal so she could get it on video.
